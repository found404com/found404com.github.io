Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026

This page is provided as a practical template and should be reviewed for your company details and local legal requirements before publication.

1. Overview

HRaw is designed to keep your workout heart rate data under your control. The app reads selected Apple Health data only with your permission and processes it locally on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains what data HRaw accesses, how it is used, and what choices you have.

2. Data HRaw may access

With your permission, HRaw may read selected Apple Health data such as workouts, heart rate samples, workout metadata, calories, distance, duration, and Apple Workout Effort Scores when available.

HRaw requests only the HealthKit permissions needed to provide workout viewing, analysis, comparison, and export features.

3. How your data is used

Your data is used to display workouts, show heart rate charts, calculate statistics and zones, compare workouts, create export files, run automatic export, and provide app features you choose to use.

4. Local processing

Health data is processed locally on your device. HRaw does not require an account and does not upload your health data to our servers.

5. Exported files

When you export data, HRaw creates CSV or JSON files on your device. You control where those files are saved or shared. If you share exported files with other apps, cloud storage, people, or services, their own privacy practices may apply.

6. Data sharing and sale

HRaw does not sell your health data. HRaw does not share your health data with advertisers, data brokers, or third parties for advertising or profiling.

7. Apple Health permissions

You can grant, deny, change, or revoke Apple Health permissions at any time in Apple Health or iOS Settings. If permissions are revoked, some HRaw features may stop working.

8. Purchases and subscription data

Paid features are processed by Apple through App Store In-App Purchases. HRaw may receive StoreKit entitlement information needed to unlock paid features, such as whether a subscription or lifetime purchase is active. Payment details are handled by Apple and are not provided to HRaw.

9. Analytics and diagnostics

HRaw may use privacy-preserving diagnostics or crash information provided by Apple or the operating system to improve app stability. This information does not include your exported heart rate files. If additional analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated.

10. Children

HRaw is not intended for use by children under the age required by local law to manage health data or App Store purchases without parental consent.

11. Data retention

Because HRaw processes health data locally, your Apple Health data remains in Apple Health unless you export it or delete it using Apple Health or iOS tools. Exported files remain wherever you save them until you delete them.

12. Security

HRaw uses platform-provided protections from iOS and Apple Health. However, no device or software system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You should protect your device and exported files appropriately.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
