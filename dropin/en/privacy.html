Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 30, 2026

1. Overview

DropIn is an iOS and Apple Watch app for recording video on your iPhone and controlling recording from Apple Watch. The app is designed to work locally on your devices and does not require an account.

This Privacy Policy explains what information DropIn may access, how it is used, and what choices you have.

2. Camera, microphone, and Photos permissions

DropIn may request access to the camera to show a live preview and record video. DropIn may request access to the microphone when audio recording is enabled. You can disable the microphone in the app.

DropIn may request access to Photos when you choose to save videos to Photos or enable auto-save to Photos. If a video is assigned to an album, DropIn may save it to a Photos album with the same name when supported by the system.

3. Videos and local gallery

Videos recorded with DropIn are saved locally inside the app unless you choose to save or share them elsewhere. The local gallery may store videos, thumbnails, durations, save status, and album assignments.

Video and audio you record may include personal information about you or other people, such as faces, voices, locations, surroundings, or activities. You are responsible for what you choose to record, save, or share.

4. Albums and settings

DropIn may store app settings on your device, including recording delay, duration limit, camera quality, focus mode, microphone setting, energy saving mode, keep-screen-awake setting, volume-button start setting, auto-save setting, album names, emoji badges, visibility, order, and selected album.

5. Apple Watch remote control

The DropIn Watch app acts as a remote control for the iPhone app. To provide this feature, DropIn may sync recording state, connection state, countdown, elapsed time, remaining time, selected album, visible album list, haptics setting, and remote-control commands between your iPhone and Apple Watch.

6. How information is used

Information is used to provide camera preview, recording, countdown, automatic stop, local gallery, album filtering, video playback, sharing, saving to Photos, Watch remote control, haptics, sounds, error alerts, and app settings you choose to use.

7. Local processing

DropIn does not require an account and does not upload your videos, audio, albums, or settings to our servers. Your content is processed locally on your devices.

8. Sharing and export

When you share a video, save it to Photos, send it to another app, or store it in a cloud service, that destination may process the video under its own privacy practices. You control when you export or share videos outside DropIn.

9. Data sharing and sale

DropIn does not sell your videos, audio, albums, or settings. DropIn does not share this information with advertisers, data brokers, or third parties for advertising or profiling.

10. Analytics and diagnostics

DropIn may use privacy-preserving diagnostics or crash information provided by Apple or the operating system to improve app stability. This information does not include your recorded videos. If additional analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated.

11. Data retention

Videos and settings remain on your device until you delete them, delete the app, or remove them using system tools. Videos saved to Photos remain in Photos until you delete them there.

12. Children

DropIn is not intended for use by children under the age required by local law to use recording apps, manage App Store downloads, or consent to recording without parental consent.

13. Security

DropIn uses platform-provided protections from iOS, watchOS, and Photos. However, no device or software system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You should protect your device, Photos library, and shared videos appropriately.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
